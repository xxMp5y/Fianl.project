Project Notes

This project was created by Ghassan and Muhannad over the course of approximately six days. The objective was to develop a simple yet functional website to demonstrate the basics of HTML and CSS, along with some additional features to enhance learning and usability.

Ghassan has experience in designing front-end interfaces, and he contributed significantly to the layout and styling of the website. Previously, Ghassan has designed tutorials for front-end design for platforms such as YouTube and Amazon, bringing valuable expertise to this project.
